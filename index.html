RESEARCH LINES OF INQUIRY

An Analytical Chasm

When acting as an ensemble in the brain, neurons of relatively low complexity exhibit extremely complex emergent behavior. How do we get from LTP/LTD to learning, attention, memory, theory of mind? Why are so many of humans' seemingly simple abstract higher order behaviors so difficult to model with the building blocks of the human brain?

Intuitive Cognitive Models

How do we model other people's mind, and how do we model the physical objects around us? What is the relationship between them? How do our intuitive judgments about people and objects give us insight into the models we have?

Knowledge Engineering

What are the implications of knowing how we think? How can we implement the insights about how the brain works into mechanistic systems? What is the best way to measure progress in artificially intelligent systems?
